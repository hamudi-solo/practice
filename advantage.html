“The Boy Who Fixed Shoes”

Advantages / Life Lessons from Abdullahi's Story

  1. Skill is Power

    2. Even without money, having a skill (like shoemaking) can help you survive and succeed.

  2. Responsibility at a Young Age

    3. Abdullahi took care of his family at only 12 — proving that maturity doesn’t come with age but with action.

  3. Education Changes Everything

    4. One opportunity for education transformed his life and gave him the tools to build his future.

  4. Hard Work Builds Respect

    5. The man helped Abdullahi because he saw his dedication — hard work attracts people who want to help

  5. From Struggles Come Strength

    6. His difficult past made him strong, humble, and grateful. Pain taught him patience.

  6. Giving Back to the Community

    7. Abdullahi didn’t forget his roots — he opened a school to lift others just like he was lifted.

  7. Business Can Start Small

    8. He started with just a needle and glue, but turned it into a real business — no need to wait for “perfect conditions.”

  8. Reuse and Innovate

    9. His idea of making shoes from recycled tires shows how creativity and problem-solving can create opportunity.

  9. Growth Takes Time

    10. It took years of learning, failing, and working before he became successful — patience is key.

  10. Stay Humble, Stay Focused

    11. Even after success, Abdullahi remained connected to his beginnings — true success is remembering who you are.
